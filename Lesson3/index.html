Site Plan

Site Description

The purpose of Huckleberry Weather is to give up to date weather for the small Idaho towns of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven.
Site visitors can view the weather forecast, a photo gallery, and storm center for large storm fronts coming to the area.

Site Name

The home page address for Huckleberry Weather is huckleberryweather.com.

Purpose Statement

Huckleberry Weather's purpose is to bring weather forecasts to rural Idaho. Small towns are often left out of weather forcasts done by large stations who mainly focus on large cities. Small towns deserve to have up to date weather predictions, so Huckleberry Weather's purpose is to fill this gap.
