Temple Spotlight

This month's temple spotlight is the Salt Lake City Temple. Our Salt Lake City location is a popular place to stay thanks to the historic Salt Lake Temple and Temple Square. At the end of December 2019, the Salt Lake Temple will be undergoing a major renovation. In this photo rendering courtesy of churchofjesuschrist.org, you can see the beautiful updates that will be made to Temple Square. Don't let this renovation keep you away from visiting us! The Salt Lake valley has 4 other temples within a 20 minute drive of our inn. Come visit us and enjoy the temples and history of the Salt Lake region. The renovations on the Salt Lake Temple are expected to finish in 2023.
